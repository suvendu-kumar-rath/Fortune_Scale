Fortune Scales & Instruments

Fortune Scales is a premier manufacturer, sales & service provider in the field of high-precision Industrial Weigh-Bridges, weighing Scales, Commercial weighing machines, Jewelry weighing machines. We design and produce reliable solutions tailored to the evolving needs of various industries. Alongside manufacturing, we provide comprehensive support services—including installation, maintenance, and technical assistance—to ensure optimal machine performance and customer satisfaction.

Weigh Bridges

Experience precision and reliability with our state-of-the-art Weigh Bridges. Our comprehensive range includes Pit Type & Pit Less Weighbridges, Mobile and Unmanned Weighbridges, designed to meet the highest standards of accuracy and durability. With capacities ranging from 50 Kg to 5000 Kg and Class-III accuracy, our weighbridges are the perfect solution for industrial weighing needs.

Our Products

Discover our extensive range of precision weighing solutions including Bench Platform Scales, Floor Platform Scales, Table Top/Counter Scales, Crane Scales, Hanging Scales, Jewelry Scales, Laboratory Scales, and more. Each product is crafted with precision engineering and built to last, ensuring accurate measurements for various industrial and commercial applications.

About Us

Brief History

Fortune Scales and Instruments was formed in the year 2006, with a view to provide high quality products and service than available in the market to cater the need of the industries, wholesale, retail and other allied markets.

Management: The Management is headed by Proprietor Mr. Pitabas Das, who had started his very own career in this field since 1971 and have vast knowledge with experience in the field of complete weighing solutions.

With a very humble beginning, Fortune Scales and Instruments has gradually climbed the ladder of success and now we are expanding our area of operations throughout the country.
