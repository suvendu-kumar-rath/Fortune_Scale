Brief History

Fortune Scales and Instruments was formed in the year 2006, with a view to provide high quality products and service than available in the market to cater the need of the industries, wholesale, retail and other allied markets.

Management: The Management is headed by Proprietor Mr. Pitabas Das, who had started his very own career in this field since 1971 and have vast knowledge with experience in the field of complete weighing solutions.

With a very humble beginning, Fortune Scales and Instruments has gradually climbed the ladder of success and now we are expanding our area of operations throughout the country.
